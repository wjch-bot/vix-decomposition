## Column Descriptions
- VIX_Computed: 30-day constant-maturity VIX using 2-expiry linear interpolation
- F1 (Sticky Strike):     ATM vol change from SPX spot move holding skew fixed
- F2 (Parallel Shift):    Full surface level change at same strike
- F3 (Put Skew Gradient): Put shoulder (30-delta) change net of F2
- F4 (Call Skew Gradient):Call shoulder (30-delta) change net of F2
- F5 (Downside Convexity):Put wing (10-delta) convexity change net of F2+F3
- F6 (Upside Convexity):  Call wing (10-delta) convexity change net of F2+F4
- VIX_Actual:             CBOE published VIX (or payload VIX spot where available)

## Methodology Notes
- F1-F6 use SINGLE-STRIKE representative approximation (30-delta put/call, 10-delta put/call)
  Full F3 requires VIX recomputed after adjusting ALL 15-45 delta put prices
  Full F5 requires VIX recomputed after adjusting ALL 1-15 delta put prices
- Delta-to-strike: iterative approach using actual IV from near-term skew at each strike
- F1 = OLD_30d_put_vol_at_NEW_spot - OLD_ATM_vol (per whitepaper P13)
